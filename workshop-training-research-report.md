## 2. Workshop Format Best Practices (2025)

### 2.1 Blended Learning Approach (Recommended)
- **Structure:** Combine online modules + in-person/live virtual sessions
- **ROI:** McKinsey study shows 30-40% higher ROI vs single-mode delivery
- **Application:** Technical teams learn theory online, apply in live workshops
- **Fits your model:** Allows quarterly updates with new online content + live sessions

### 2.2 Interactive & Hands-On Design
- **Group Size:** Maximum 50 participants per workshop for personal feedback
- **Format:** Expert-led with real use cases, participants build solutions themselves
- **Engagement:** Extremely high interaction with real-time problem-solving
- **Tools:** Participants work with actual AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, n8n)

### 2.3 Human-Centered Problem-Solving
- **Customization:** Participants bring their own work challenges
- **Practical Focus:** Address specific organizational needs, not just theory
- **Time-to-Value:** Single-day workshops that deliver immediate applicable skills
- **Follow-up:** Quarterly check-ins for updated AI trends (supports your recurring model)

### 2.4 Essential Workshop Components

**Core Modules (3-8 standardized sessions):**
1. AI Fundamentals & Landscape
2. Prompt Engineering Mastery
3. Workflow Audit & AI Opportunity Identification
4. Hands-on Tool Training (ChatGPT, Claude, n8n)
5. Automation Blueprint Development
6. Data Privacy & AI Ethics
7. Implementation Planning
8. Advanced Applications (Optional)

**Customization Layer (1-2 sessions):**
- Industry-specific use cases
- Company workflow integration
- Custom automation templates

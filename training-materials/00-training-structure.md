# AI Workshop Training - Complete Structure

## Training Philosophy
**Goal:** Enable participants to use AI to save 10+ hours per week within 30 days through practical, hands-on training that addresses real business problems.

**Approach:**
- 70% hands-on practice, 30% theory
- Every concept demonstrated with real examples
- Participants create their own solutions during training
- Focus on immediate ROI, not abstract concepts

## Video Production Specifications

### **Standard Format (All Videos)**
- Length: 10-30 min per video (sweet spot: 15 min)
- Resolution: 1080p minimum
- Format: Screen recording + talking head (picture-in-picture)
- Captions: English (auto-generated, edited for accuracy)
- Chapters: Time-stamped sections for easy navigation

### **Video Structure (Every Video)**
1. **Hook (30 sec):** What you'll learn + why it matters + ROI promise
2. **Overview (1 min):** 3-5 key points covered
3. **Content (70% of time):** Teaching with examples
4. **Demo/Practice (20% of time):** Hands-on application
5. **Summary (1 min):** Key takeaways + next steps
6. **CTA (30 sec):** Download template, try exercise, next video

### **Engagement Elements**
- On-screen graphics for key concepts
- Real-time screen demos (not just slides)
- Pause points for practice
- Downloadable resources mentioned in video
- Question prompts for reflection

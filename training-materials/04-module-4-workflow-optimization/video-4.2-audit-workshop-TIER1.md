### [3:30-4:00] STEP 2: SCORE TASKS (You do: 10 minutes)

**Your task:** Score each task on 3 criteria (1-5 scale each).

**The 3 criteria:**
1. **Time Spent** (per week): 5 = >2hr, 4 = 1-2hr, 3 = 30-60min, 2 = 15-30min, 1 = <15min
2. **AI Suitability**: 5 = 80-100% AI can do, 4 = 50-80%, 3 = 25-50%, 2 = 10-25%, 1 = <10%
3. **Repetitiveness**: 5 = Daily, 4 = 3-5x/week, 3 = Weekly, 2 = 2-3x/month, 1 = Monthly

**Instructions:**
1. Go through each task on your list
2. Score Time, AI, Repetitiveness
3. Calculate TOTAL (sum of 3 scores)
4. Estimate time AI could save

**Tips for AI Suitability:**
- **High (4-5):** Drafting, summarizing, research, data analysis, brainstorming
- **Medium (2-3):** Strategic work, quality review, relationship communication
- **Low (1):** Final decisions, sensitive conversations, physical work

**This takes 10 minutes. Go through systematically.**

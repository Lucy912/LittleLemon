### [3:00-4:30] STEP 2: SCORE AI POTENTIAL

**Goal:** Determine which tasks AI can actually help with.

**The 3 scoring criteria (1-5 scale for each):**

**Criteria 1: Time Spent**
- 5 = Takes >2 hours per week
- 4 = Takes 1-2 hours per week
- 3 = Takes 30 min - 1 hour per week
- 2 = Takes 15-30 min per week
- 1 = Takes <15 min per week

**Criteria 2: AI Suitability**
- 5 = AI can do 80-100% of this task
- 4 = AI can do 50-80%
- 3 = AI can do 25-50%
- 2 = AI can do 10-25%
- 1 = AI can barely help (<10%)

**Criteria 3: Repetitiveness**
- 5 = Do this daily
- 4 = Do this 3-5x per week
- 3 = Do this weekly
- 2 = Do this 2-3x per month
- 1 = Do this monthly or less

**How to assess AI suitability (Criteria 2):**

**High AI suitability (4-5):**

**Create priority tiers:**

### [7:15-7:45] COMMON AUDIT MISTAKES

**Mistake 1: Too Many Tasks at Once**
âŒ Trying to AI-optimize 20 tasks in week 1
âœ… Start with 3-5 quick wins, build from there

**Why:** Habit formation requires focus. Better to do 5 tasks well than 20 poorly.

---

**Mistake 2: Starting with Hardest Tasks**
âŒ Beginning with complex, high-stakes work
âœ… Start with low-risk, high-frequency tasks

**Why:** Quick wins build confidence and skills before tackling harder tasks.

---

**Mistake 3: No Measurement**
âŒ "I think AI is helping..."
âœ… "AI saved me 4.5 hours this week on email responses"

**Why:** Measurement shows ROI, motivates continuation, identifies what works.

---

**Mistake 4: Perfect Prompts on First Try**
âŒ Expecting immediate perfection
âœ… Iterating and refining over 2-3 weeks

**Why:** AI workflows improve with practice and adjustment.

---

**Mistake 5: Audit and Forget**
âŒ Complete audit, never look at it again
âœ… Review weekly, update monthly, re-audit quarterly

**Why:** Work changes, AI capabilities evolve, new opportunities emerge.

## Production Notes

### Visual Style:
- Clean, minimal graphics
- Consistent brand colors (choose 2-3)
- Sans-serif font for on-screen text (large, readable)
- High contrast for readability

### Audio:
- Clear narration (invest in decent mic)
- Light background music (20% volume)
- Music picks up energy at 6:28
- Quick sound effects for pattern interrupts (subtle)

### Editing:
- Cut all pauses longer than 2 seconds
- Add zoom effects when showing specific text
- Highlight text being typed in ChatGPT
- Color-code the 5 framework parts consistently

### Accessibility:
- Captions for entire video (edited for accuracy)
- High-contrast text
- Visual + audio for all key points

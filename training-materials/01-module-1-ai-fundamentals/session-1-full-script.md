## SCRIPT NOTES FOR PRESENTER

**Tone & Energy:**
- Enthusiastic but not over-the-top
- Conversational, like talking to a friend
- Confident but not condescending
- Encouraging - you want them to succeed

**Pacing:**
- Moderate speed (not too fast)
- Pause after key points to let them land
- Vary your pace - speed up for excitement, slow down for emphasis

**Delivery Tips:**
- Smile (it shows in your voice)
- Use hand gestures (but keep them in frame)
- Make eye contact with camera during talking head sections
- Don't read the script word-for-word - internalize it, then speak naturally
- If you mess up, just stop, take a breath, and start the sentence again

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Monotone delivery
- Reading directly from script (sounds robotic)
- Talking too fast (viewers need time to absorb)
- Forgetting to pause (overwhelms viewer)
- Over-editing (some natural speaking patterns are good)

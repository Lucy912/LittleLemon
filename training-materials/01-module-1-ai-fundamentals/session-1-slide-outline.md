## Design Guidelines

### Typography:
- **Headlines:** Sans-serif, bold, 60-80pt
- **Body text:** Sans-serif, regular, 32-48pt
- **Accent text:** Sans-serif, medium, 24-36pt
- **Font recommendation:** Inter, Helvetica, Roboto, or Montserrat

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary:** Your brand color (e.g., blue #2563EB)
- **Secondary:** Complement (e.g., purple #7C3AED)
- **Accent:** Bright (e.g., green #10B981 for success, red #EF4444 for problem)
- **Background:** White or very light gray (#F9FAFB)
- **Text:** Dark gray or black (#111827)

### Visual Style:
- Minimal - lots of white space
- High contrast for readability
- Consistent icons throughout
- Framework colors: Keep same 5 colors for Role/Task/Context/Format/Tone
- Max 3-4 colors per slide
- Avoid clip art - use simple icons or custom graphics

### Slide Rhythm:
- Alternate between text-heavy and visual-heavy slides
- No slide with more than 5 bullet points
- Use builds/animations sparingly (mainly for framework reveal)
- Keep consistent header/footer if using

---

## Production Notes

### Software Options:
- **Keynote** (Mac) - best for design control
- **PowerPoint** (Windows/Mac) - universal compatibility
- **Google Slides** - collaborative, free, accessible anywhere
- **Canva** - easiest for non-designers, templates available
- **Figma** - most design flexibility, learning curve

### Export Formats:
- **For video:** Export as PNG images (1920x1080), import into video editor
- **For sharing:** PDF (universal) or native format (editable)
- **For web:** Individual slide images or animated GIFs

### Time Estimates:
- **Using template:** 2-3 hours to customize
- **From scratch:** 5-8 hours for polished design
- **Quick version:** 1 hour with basic design

---

## Slide Template Structure

Each slide should have:
1. **Consistent header:** Title in same location (top left or centered)
2. **Content area:** Main body (centered or left-aligned)
3. **Optional footer:** Slide number, brand mark, or social handle
4. **Margins:** Minimum 100px from edges (safe zone for projection)

---

## Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] Text size minimum 32pt for body text
- [ ] High contrast ratio (4.5:1 minimum for text)
- [ ] Color is not the only way information is conveyed
- [ ] Alt text for images (if sharing digital version)
- [ ] Readable fonts (avoid script/decorative)
- [ ] Sufficient white space (not cramped)
